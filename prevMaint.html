Nationwide Preventative Maintenance

Nothing impacts productivity more than equipment breakdowns. Proactively checking your assets is one of the best ways to stay ahead of time-consuming, expensive repairs.



ACES offers a full preventative maintenance program. We’ll check the pressure settings and hydraulic fluid levels on your compactors and balers and provide any necessary service. We’ll check your metal machines to make sure that any moving parts are not suffering from metal fatigue. This kind of preventative maintenance is a cost-effective way to extend the life of your baler and compactor and may save you a considerable amount of money in the long run.

ACES also offers professional cleaning services. Every year, our technician will clean, deodorize and disinfect your compactor and chute. We also clean of the hopper door and we check and adjust the alignment and tension.
